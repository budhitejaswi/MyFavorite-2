My Favorite Hobby
Reading Books

I love reading! The first reason is because it brings a story to life and when I am reading I feel like I am in the story like a ghost watching the story happen and I am in a different world and I am invisible to the real world. Another one is that it inspires me to use my imagination and I learn many new things. Reading helps me "escape reality" and go into a whole new world. Also whenever I am feeling sad I pick a good book and I immediately feel happy and I think that's because I can relate to at least one of the characters. I also love to see how the character's delevop and learn to grow and how much they have grown from the beginning.
